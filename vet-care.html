What to Expect at Your Visit

1

Check-In

Arrive 10 minutes early to complete any paperwork. Our friendly staff will greet you and your pet.

2

Examination

Our veterinarian will perform a thorough physical exam and discuss your pet's health history.

3

Treatment Plan

We'll explain our findings and recommend a personalized treatment or preventive care plan.

4

Questions

We encourage questions! Our team will answer all your concerns about your pet's health and care.

5

Follow-Up

We'll schedule any necessary follow-up appointments and provide take-home care instructions.

6

Checkout

Emergency Services Available 24/7

If your pet is experiencing a medical emergency, call us immediately at 1-888-332-7387. Our emergency veterinary team is available around the clock to provide urgent care when your pet needs it most.

Common emergencies include: severe bleeding, difficulty breathing, seizures, suspected poisoning, severe vomiting/diarrhea, inability to urinate, eye injuries, or collapse.
