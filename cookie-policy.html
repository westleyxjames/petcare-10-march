Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

FurryFriends CareServices uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt-out of these cookies.

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Authentication cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security cookies: Help protect against fraudulent use of login credentials

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance.

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website traffic and user behavior
  • Page load time: Monitors website performance
  • Error tracking: Helps us identify and fix technical issues

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, personalized features.

  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Language cookies: Store your language selection
  • Cookie consent: Remember your cookie preferences

4. Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.

  • Retargeting cookies: Show you relevant ads after visiting our site
  • Social media cookies: Enable sharing of content on social platforms
  • Ad tracking: Measure effectiveness of advertising campaigns

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Website Functions: Enable core features like booking appointments and processing payments
  • User Experience: Remember your preferences and provide personalized content
  • Analytics: Understand how visitors use our site to improve functionality and content
  • Marketing: Deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness
  • Security: Protect against fraudulent activity and enhance website security
  • Communication: Facilitate customer service and support interactions

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics and deliver advertisements. These third parties include:

  • Google Analytics: Web analytics service that tracks and reports website traffic
  • Google Ads: Advertising platform for displaying relevant ads
  • Facebook Pixel: Analytics tool for measuring ad effectiveness
  • Social Media Plugins: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter integration
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment gateway cookies

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We do not control these cookies and recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner asking for your permission to use cookies. You can:

  • Accept: Allow all cookies for optimal website experience
  • Decline: Only essential cookies will be used
  • Learn More: Read this cookie policy for detailed information

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience on our website. You can typically:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Instructions for managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies

Opting Out of Targeted Advertising

If you wish to opt-out of targeted advertising, you can do so through:

  • Google Ads Settings: Manage your Google ad preferences
  • Facebook Ad Preferences: Control Facebook ads
  • Digital Advertising Alliance: Opt-out of interest-based advertising
  • Network Advertising Initiative: Opt-out of network advertising

Please note that opting out does not mean you will no longer see advertisements—it means the ads you see will be less relevant to your interests.

Cookie Duration

Cookies can be classified by their duration:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted. Our persistent cookies typically last between 30 days and 2 years

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that signals to websites that you don't want to have your online activities tracked. Our website currently does not respond to Do Not Track signals, but you can still manage cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, please be aware that:

  • Some features of our website may not function properly
  • You may not be able to book appointments or access certain services
  • Your preferences and settings will not be saved
  • You may need to re-enter information during each visit
  • Your experience may be less personalized

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
