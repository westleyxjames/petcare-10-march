Our Services

Comprehensive pet care solutions tailored to meet your furry friend's unique needs

Dog Walking

Our professional dog walkers provide daily exercise and companionship for your furry friends. Whether your dog needs a quick potty break or an extended adventure, we tailor each walk to your pet's energy level and preferences.

What's Included:

  • 15, 30, or 60-minute walks
  • GPS tracking of routes
  • Fresh water provided
  • Photo updates after each walk
  • Basic training reinforcement

Perfect For:

  • Busy professionals
  • High-energy dogs
  • Puppies learning leash manners
  • Senior dogs needing gentle exercise

Pet Sitting

Travel with confidence knowing your pets are cared for in the comfort of their own home. Our experienced pet sitters provide personalized care, maintain your pet's routine, and give you peace of mind while you're away.

Services Include:

  • Feeding and fresh water
  • Playtime and exercise
  • Medication administration
  • Litter box maintenance
  • Mail and plant care
  • Home security checks

Ideal For:

  • Cats who prefer staying home
  • Multiple pets
  • Pets with special needs
  • Extended vacations

Pet Day Care

Give your social pet a fun-filled day of supervised play, exercise, and socialization. Our daycare facility provides a safe, stimulating environment where dogs can make friends and burn energy while you work or run errands.

Daily Activities:

  • Group play sessions
  • Individual attention time
  • Rest periods with calming music
  • Enrichment activities
  • Basic training exercises

Great For:

  • Social, energetic dogs
  • Puppies learning social skills
  • Dogs who get lonely at home
  • Working pet parents

Overnight Pet Care

For pets who need round-the-clock attention, our overnight care services provide comfort and security. A dedicated caregiver stays with your pet through the night, ensuring they feel safe, loved, and well-cared for.

Includes:

  • Overnight supervision
  • Evening and morning meals
  • Bedtime routine
  • Multiple potty breaks
  • Cuddles and companionship
  • Emergency vet transport if needed

Best For:

  • Anxious pets
  • Pets with medical needs
  • Senior pets requiring extra attention
  • Overnight business trips

Pet Grooming

Keep your pet looking and feeling their best with our professional grooming services. Our experienced groomers provide gentle, stress-free grooming in a calm environment, tailored to your pet's breed and coat type.

Services Offered:

  • Bath and blow dry
  • Haircut and styling
  • Nail trimming and filing
  • Ear cleaning
  • Teeth brushing
  • De-shedding treatments

Perfect For:

  • All breeds and coat types
  • Regular maintenance
  • Special occasions
  • Nervous or anxious pets

Pet Training

Build a stronger bond with your pet through positive reinforcement training. Our certified trainers work with dogs of all ages to teach basic obedience, address behavioral issues, and develop good manners.

Training Programs:

  • Basic obedience (sit, stay, come)
  • Leash training
  • House training
  • Socialization skills
  • Behavior modification
  • Advanced commands and tricks

Ideal For:

  • Puppies and young dogs
  • Rescue dogs
  • Dogs with behavioral issues
  • First-time pet owners
